# FNP-Data-Analysis-And-DashBoard
Overview
This report presents a comprehensive sales analysis for FNP (Ferns N Petals) based on 1,000 orders processed. The key performance indicators (KPIs) assessed include total revenue, average customer spending, order delivery time, and revenue distribution across various factors such as occasions, product categories, time of order, and location.

Key Performance using below skils

Power qurey for data clening 
power pivot for data modeling 
pivot table for data visualization

Revenue Analysis
Revenue by Occasions
The revenue generated varies across different occasions, with Anniversaries and Raksha Bandhan contributing significantly to total sales. Other high-revenue occasions include Holi and Valentine's Day, while Diwali and Birthday sales show moderate revenue figures.

Revenue by Category
Product categories align closely with occasion-based trends. The Anniversary and Raksha Bandhan categories perform best, followed by Holi and Birthday-related categories. Lower-performing categories need targeted marketing strategies to improve sales.

Revenue by Month
Sales exhibit a seasonal trend, with peak revenue observed in July (coinciding with Raksha Bandhan). February and November also show high revenue, likely due to Valentine’s Day and Diwali sales, respectively. March and April reflect lower revenue figures, indicating a possible need for seasonal promotional campaigns.

Revenue by Top Products
The best-performing products include:

Colors – Highest revenue generator
Soft Toys & Sweets – Consistently popular
Cake – Moderate sales
Mugs & Plants – Lower revenue contribution
Revenue by Hour (Order Time)
The peak order times are between 8 AM - 10 AM and 6 PM - 8 PM, indicating higher purchase activity in the morning and evening hours.
A dip in sales is observed between 12 PM - 2 PM, suggesting potential for midday promotional strategies.
Geographic Insights
The top 10 cities contributing to orders include:

Imphal and Kavali – Highest number of orders
Bharora, Bichomager, and Dibrugarh – Consistently strong order volume
Gunakal, Haridwar, and North Dumdum – Moderate sales performance
These insights suggest potential for targeted campaigns in high-performing regions while boosting visibility in underperforming cities.
